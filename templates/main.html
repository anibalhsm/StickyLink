Why Choose Sticky Linerless Labels?

Sticky Linerless Labels are designed for situations where only an integrated printer mechanism, panel mounted unit, or even a battery-powered portable device is available.

Overcoming Challenges

Traditional label printing faces challenges with label registration and roll length. However, Sticky rolls are linerless and self-adhesive, eliminating the need for any registration mechanism. This makes them ideal for printing variable length labels, such as test reports.

Full-Length Rolls

Unlike traditional label rolls, Sticky rolls have no backing, allowing for full-length rolls to be employed. This eliminates the fiddly peeling process associated with traditional labels.

Special Adhesive

The rolls we supply have a specially formulated adhesive on the rear face of the paper. This adhesive doesn’t adhere to the coated front surface of the roll, so it won’t stick to itself! It’s also designed not to migrate onto the printer’s roller or the delicate thermal printhead.
